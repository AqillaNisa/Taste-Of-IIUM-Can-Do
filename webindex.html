About Taste of IIUM

Taste of IIUM is a web-based platform developed to help IIUM students and visitors explore food menus and cafe locations available in different Mahallahs.

The system allows users to search food items, browse stalls, and identify food locations easily within the IIUM campus.

Our platform is designed to improve accessibility and convenience, helping you easily explore food options across all Mahallah cafes in IIUM.

  • Comprehensive Food Menus: View detailed menus, prices, and food descriptions for stalls across every Mahallah.
  • Interactive Maps & Locations: Easily pinpoint the exact coordinates of your desired food locations to reduce time spent searching.
  • Smart Search & Browsing: Quickly search for specific food items, browse available stalls, and check operating hours.

Discover food areas across IIUM Mahallahs.

Our interactive map feature is designed to help users easily identify the location of their desired food within the IIUM campus.

  • Display accurate Mahallah locations on the campus map.
  • Show exact map coordinates for specific cafeterias.
  • Organize food stalls perfectly according to their Mahallah location.

By visually mapping out the dining options, we aim to improve convenience and reduce the time you spend searching for food.

Search and browse your favorite local stalls.

Whether you know what you want or are just browsing, finding the right food stall has never been easier.

  • Search for specific food items, stalls, or Mahallahs easily
  • View comprehensive stall information including captivating stall images.
  • Check real-time operating hours so you never walk to a closed cafe.

Explore comprehensive and mouth-watering menus.

Get a clear picture of what each stall offers. We provide clear and organized information about the types of food available.

    • View food names alongside detailed, appetizing descriptions.
    • Check clear pricing to help manage your student budget.
    • Browse food images to see exactly what you are ordering.

    Each food item is strictly categorized under its specific stall, making menu navigation seamless and intuitive.

Secure access and robust management tools.

Taste of IIUM is designed with a strong backend to support both everyday users and system administrators.

  • Safe registration and login for all users.
  • Differentiates between normal user access and administrative controls.
  • Admins can easily add, edit, or delete food items and stall details.
